About Tayssir

Tayssir is an innovative Algerian startup that empowers women to gain confidence behind the wheel. Our app connects women drivers with certified female instructors for real-life or virtual training sessions. With personalized coaching, VR simulators, and safe vehicle rental options, Tayssir makes driving practice accessible and stress-free.

Our Mission

To support women’s autonomy and safety on the road by providing a digital learning ecosystem that combines technology, pedagogy, and trust.

Innovation

  • Integration of VR driving simulators for immersive learning.
  • A female marketplace for promoting products and services safely.
  • AI-based coaching suggestions tailored to each driver.
